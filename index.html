About MGKVP Varanasi

Mahatma Gandhi Kashi Vidyapith (MGKVP), located in the historic city of Varanasi, is one of India's premier institutions for higher education. Established in 1921, the university has a strong legacy of academic excellence and social commitment. Over the years, MGKVP has nurtured generations of scholars, intellectuals, and professionals, contributing to various fields of knowledge and societal progress.

In the field of Journalism and Mass Communication, MGKVP has emerged as a significant center for media studies, offering a dynamic curriculum that blends theoretical understanding with practical exposure. The department is dedicated to producing skilled professionals who can navigate the rapidly evolving media industry, covering areas such as print journalism, digital media, public relations, broadcasting, and film studies.
With experienced faculty, state-of-the-art media labs, and collaborations with industry experts, the program equips students with the necessary skills to excel in the competitive media landscape.

During my tenure as a BA (Hons.) Mass Communication student at MGKVP, I actively engaged in various academic and extracurricular activities that enhanced my understanding of media and communication.
I had the opportunity to participate in hands-on projects, including news reporting, video production, radio broadcasting, and digital content creation.
I also contributed to student-led media initiatives, attended workshops with leading journalists and filmmakers, and gained practical exposure through internships with media houses and PR firms.

Additionally, I played a role in organizing media events, seminars, and awareness campaigns, further strengthening my leadership and communication skills.
My time at MGKVP allowed me to explore the power of storytelling, the ethics of journalism, and the impact of media on society, shaping me into a confident and industry-ready media professional.

As I reflect on my journey at MGKVP, I look forward to applying these experiences in the real world, continuing to learn, grow, and contribute to the ever-changing landscape of mass communication.
